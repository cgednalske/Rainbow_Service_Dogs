Find a Suitable Service Dog

We work with animal control centers, Humane Societies, and several local rescues to obtain suitable animals for participants.

All potential animals are tested for aggression, noise response, and other behavior issues prior to adoption. The animals must display non-aggression to other animals.

Individuals are responsible for all adopted animals, their adoption fees, shots and spaying/neutering.

Train Your Service Dog

120+ hours of physical and social training for dog and handler, 40+ hours of community outings, and 40+ hours of instruction.

Learn to Train Your Service Dog

The program includes 120+ hours of physical and social training of the dog and handler, 40+ hours of community outings, and 40+ hours of classroom instruction.

We include all AKC testing fees and classes. AKC filing fees for each test are the responsibility of the handler as well as the cost of all patches for each test. Fees range from $15.00 to $25.00 plus $19.00 for AKC CANINE PARTNERS registration number (if your dog is not already AKC registered) and patches are $12.00.

Upon entrance into the program, members will receive a welcome packet containing class schedules, sample training logs, sample homework sheets and a list of rules and expectations.

Our program offers a sliding scale for all training fees which is based on income. Training costs run between $12.50 to $18.50 an (50 min) hour for groups, and $18.00 to $25.00 on private. In-home visits range up to $30.00 an hour.

Payment for the first three months are due upon entry to the program and funds are non-refundable.
