Meet Our Dogs

Meet our incredible pup graduates and see how they complete their training.

Our Rainbow Service Dog Graduates go on to help their owners with a variety of mental and physical illnesses and disabilities. We train our dogs to detect changes in the body or in their surroundings and alert their owners instantly. Having a service dog can be life-changing, promoting healing, empowerment and most importantly, independence which in most cases, improves the quality of life.

Training Gallery

HOME DEPOT

Our groups often go on outings to different business to practice their training. Here they are on a visit to the local Home Depot, where they worked on stays and settles in the aisles.

AIRPORT OUTINGS

Our groups often take trips to the airport to practice training methods. It's helpful for our more advanced dogs to work on their skills in busy areas with more distractions.

ALWAYS PRACTICING

Tink, Zoe, and Molly practicing “leave it” and “down stay.” It takes approximately 12-18 months to reach the level needed for our dogs to begin assisting their owners on their own.

POSITIVE PROGRAMS

Our program uses positive training methods only. We believe that negative training is harmful and ineffective for both the dog and owner, and can be counted on to keep our training positive.

OUR TRAINERS

Our magnificent trainers have decades of experience training service dogs. They have worked with all breeds and sizes and are some of the best in the business!

TRAINING STRUCTURE

Our training consists of class with both you and your dog in quality one-on-one and group time. We train you to handle and work with your dog from the very beginning, making the transition to full assistance as easy as possible.
