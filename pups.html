Meet Our Dogs

Meet our incredible pup graduates and see how they complete their training.

Our Raibow Service Dog Graduates go on to help their owners with a variety of mental and physical illnesses and disabilities. We train our dogs to detect changes in the body or in their surroundings and alert their owners instantly. Having a service dog can be life-changing, promoting healing, empowerment and most importantly, independence which in most cases, improves the quality of life.
